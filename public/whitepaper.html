1. Executive Summary

$HAD (Have A Dream) is a community-driven utility token inspired by Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. and the principle of equal participation in collective creation. $HAD serves as the key to haveadream.xyz features — governance voting, dream-sharing tools, meme generators, and future inspiration integrations.

No yields, profits, or financial returns are promised. $HAD is for platform access and governance participation — not an investment product.

Fixed supply of 10 billion $HAD (minted January 2024), zero tax, transparent allocations. Official launch: January 15, 2027. #community #utility #meme — Respect to MLK

2. Vision & Mission

Vision

A world where creative tools and governance are accessible to every dreamer — open participation, transparent community direction, and long-term platform utility.

Mission

Build haveadream.xyz where $HAD holders govern, create, share dreams, and shape the movement — honoring Dr. King's unity while embracing meme culture and decentralized community power.
